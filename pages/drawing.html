My Drawing Journey

How I began drawing?

I seriously started drawing 8 years ago. Before that I drew occasionally without necessarily trying to improve. For the past 8 years I've been trying to understand how to draw a person, and I've also been learning color theory so that I can compose a beautiful image. Although I draw purely out of passion, I've found that doing so has enabled me to develop my artistic vision of things, which can prove useful, especially in the field of web development!
